## Mood & voice (author notes)

**Mood / vibe:** Fresh, trustworthy SaaS — generous whitespace, one saturated blue used with restraint, soft layered shadows. Not playful; calm and precise.

**Component principles:** surfaces are white cards on a warm gray page; elevation via the two shadow steps, never borders alone; the gradient is reserved for the hero.
